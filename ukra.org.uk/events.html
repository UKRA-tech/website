Upcoming Events

UK rocketry clubs have regular and frequent launches.  Refer to the club map at http://www.ukra.org.uk/ukclubs and the clubs own website for location, meeting dates and further details.

Note that many clubs change their regular flying slot due to weather conditions, landowner activities (e.g. harvesting, fertilising or seeding) or individual club needs.  Check the club website and Facebook page before travelling to ensure that the event is still happening. SARA usually have a roll over date a couple of weeks later. To submit an event for this page please use the webmaster contact form.

First Sunday of the month: East Anglian Rocketry Society (EARS) http://www.ears.org.uk/

Second Sunday of the month: Midland Rocketry Club (MRC) http://www.midlandrocketry.org.uk/

Third Sunday of the month: Fins Over Gwent (FOG) https://fogrocketry.bmfa.club/ and Scottish Aeronautics and Rocketry Association http://www.sara.rocketry.org.uk/

Large/overnight launch events for 2022:

BigEARS, East Anglian Rocketry Society (http://www.ears.org.uk/): Saturday 30th April to Monday 2nd May

Midland club weekend (MiniSky), Midland Rocketry Club (https://www.midlandrocketry.org.uk/): Saturday 11th June to Sunday 12th June

International Rocket Week (IRW), Scottish Aeronautics and Rocketry Association http://www.sara.rocketry.org.uk/: Monday 22nd August to Monday 29th August

Midland Sky, Midland Rocketry Club (https://www.midlandrocketry.org.uk/): Friday 16th September to Sunday 18th September
