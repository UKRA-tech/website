UKRA Annual General Meeting - 2022

Submitted by Stuart on Thu, 22/09/2022 - 15:50

United Kingdom Rocketry Association Annual General Meeting (2022)

Date/time: 8PM Wednesday, 16th November 2022

Method: Zoom meeting (link to follow)

 

Dear UKRA Members,

The UKRA Council is pleased to announce the 2022 Annual General Meeting. Following the record attendance at last year’s meeting we will again be holding the meeting online via Zoom.

The link will be posted, and provided directly to all UKRA Members via email, prior to the meeting with the agenda and any other papers.

Nominations for Council positions are requested – to be communicated to the Secretary prior to the AGM, or at the AGM. The Constitution provides for eight Council Members.

At present we have eight members –

Chair – Paul Carter (Safety & Technical Committee Member)
Vice Chair – Stuart Livings
Secretary – Andy Calver (Safety & Technical Committee Member)
Treasurer – Mike Hoffman
Ronald Hatcher
Ben Jarvis
Aaron McNeil
Daniel Innes (resigned September 2022
Ian Lewis (from September 2022)

The AGM can hear, consider, and vote on alterations for changes to the Constitution.

Proposals for any such changes should be communicated to the Secretary in writing (letter or e-mail, or letter attached with e-mail) at least two (2) weeks prior to the AGM.

For full details pertaining to the AGM arrangements and rules – which include provision for on-line AGM and voting – please see Section 5 of the UKRA Constitution at - http://ukra.org.uk/aboutus

Please support UKRA and its purposes through active participation in this year’s AGM.

This announcement will also be sent to UKRA members by e-mail to the address we hold. If you have changed your email address and not informed UKRA please contact membership@ukra.org.uk

Andy Calver
UKRA Secretary
