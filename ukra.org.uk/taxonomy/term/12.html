Midland Sky 2021 National Rockery Weekend Event

Submitted by PaulCarter on Wed, 15/09/2021 - 11:10

Midland Sky National Rocket Weekend Event is Almost Here!!!!

Dates: 18th – 19th September 2021
Camping and set up from 5.30pm on the Friday night (17th)

On-site vendor = Wizard Rocketry & Rockets & Things

On Site Facilities: Camping, Toilets, Marquee + Prep Area, Plus Free Hot Water, Free Tables, Free Marque, Free parking (all subject to availability)
We hope very much that you will come and join us here at our excellent flying site in the Midlands!!!
