Submitted by Stuart on Thu, 22/09/2022 - 15:50

United Kingdom Rocketry Association Annual General Meeting (2022)

Date/time: 8PM Wednesday, 16th November 2022

Method: Zoom meeting (link to follow)

 

Dear UKRA Members,

The UKRA Council is pleased to announce the 2022 Annual General Meeting. Following the record attendance at last year’s meeting we will again be holding the meeting online via Zoom.

The link will be posted, and provided directly to all UKRA Members via email, prior to the meeting with the agenda and any other papers.
